# Physical Constants for Thermodynamic Simulation

This document provides the necessary physical constants for simulating
heat transfer between common substances.

## Water/Ice
- substance: water
- state: liquid
- specific_heat_capacity_J_per_kg_C: 4186
- latent_heat_of_fusion_J_per_kg: 334000

## Air
- substance: air
- state: gas
- specific_heat_capacity_J_per_kg_C: 718
- density_kg_per_m3: 1.225

## Room
- name: sealed_room
- type: container
- volume_m3: 27 # A 3m x 3m x 3m room
- insulated: true
